## How We Work

**Structure:** Project-based engagements. No retainers at launch.  
**Team model:** Co-led. Shared ownership. No single point of failure.  
**Billing:** Project rate, split evenly unless scoped otherwise.  
**Timeline:** Fast. We're built for pressure. Weeks, not quarters.

### Typical Engagement Flow

1. **Intake** — Client fills brief form. We review fit.
2. **Discovery call** — 30–45 min. Scope, timeline, budget.
3. **Proposal + SOW** — Deliverables, milestones, payment terms.
4. **Contract + deposit** — Signed MSA + 50% upfront.
5. **Kickoff** — Aligned on brief, stakeholders, delivery format.
6. **Design sprints** — Weekly check-ins, async feedback loops.
7. **Delivery** — Final files, handoff doc, offboarding.
